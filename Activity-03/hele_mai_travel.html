Oahu, Hawai'i

    Packing List

  • Travel: ID, Wallet, Boarding Pass, Charger, Camera, Prescriptions
  • Beach: Swimsuits, Beach Bag, Towel, Waterbottle
  • Clothes: Shorts,Pants, T-Shirts, Blouse, PJ, Sundress, Rain Jacket,Sandels, Water Shoes, Socks
  • Accessories: Sunglasses, Hat, Purse, Jewelry, Backpack

    Week Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arive in Oahu, Check into Hotel, Shopping at Ala Moana and dinner at Duke's Waikiki
  2. Day 2: Drive around Island, Stopping at the Koko Head, Pali Lookout, Makapu'u Lighthouse Trail
  3. Day 3: Rest Day, Check out the beach and enjoy the weather and pool at the hotel
  4. Day 4: Check out of Hotel, Head up to the North Shore of Oahu, Turtle Bay Resort, surf lessons and dinner at Roy's Beach House
  5. Day 5: Explore Kualoa Ranch ATV tours, and spend the afternoon and evening at the Polynesian Cultural Center
  6. Day 6: Morning swim and hike at Waimea Valley, spend the rest of the evening in Haleiwa shopping and exploring
  7. Day 7: Drive to airport, Departure
